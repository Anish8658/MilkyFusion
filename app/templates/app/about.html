Our Story

A journey of passion for pure, fresh dairy products

MIlky Fusion is a public limited company having its registered and corporate office at Sakhigopal village in Puri district. Incorporated in 2018 with production commencing in 2023, we've grown to become one of the fastest-growing public listed companies in Puri city.

Our commitment to quality and freshness has made us a trusted name in every household we serve. We believe in delivering not just products, but the goodness of nature in its purest form.
